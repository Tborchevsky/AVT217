HALLOWEEN

Summary: Taking place 40 years after the events of the 1978 Original Halloween, this new direct followup wipes the slate clean of the past 10 sequels.

Mostly it's the troubled times we live in that allows this energizing, elemental horror film to touch a raw nerve for #MeToo
Peter Travers
Rolling Stone

While it doesn’t capture the magic of the original, this Halloween brings much-needed closure to a troubled franchise, with Curtis excellent and Michael Myers pleasingly terrifying again.

Empire

Finally, Hollywood has made a good “Halloween” sequel. It took them only 40 years.
Johnny Oleksinski
New York Post

WIDOWS

Summary: Widows is a crime epic which sees the widows of a group of robbers come together to pull off one last heist.

McQueen creates a hypnotic panorama of everyday corruption that feels less like a crime caper than like something out of a Robert Altman film or a Richard Price novel.
Owen Gliberman

The movie, believe it or not, gives pleasure. It’s a stark, violent, cynical but thoroughly entertaining caper picture.
David Edelstein

Steve McQueen directs Widows with such aplomb and style that even the occasional large leaps of faith that the plot and storyline demand from the audience seem a small price to pay for such delights.
Rajesh Thind
